Privacy Policy

SteerIn helps users manage vehicle maintenance, trip tracking, and workshop records. We believe in complete transparency about how your data is collected, used, and protected.

Last updated: May 2026 App version: Release Candidate
🔒

Your privacy matters. SteerIn is designed with a local-first approach. Your data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to enable cloud sync.

📄 Data Collected

  • Vehicle profile data entered by the user.
  • Maintenance history, notifications, expense records, and trip summaries created inside the app.
  • Location data only while GPS trip tracking is actively used by the user.
  • Camera input only when the user scans an odometer or adds vehicle media.

⚙ How Data Is Used

  • To show vehicle health, maintenance reminders, trip summaries, and local history.
  • To support optional cloud authentication, backup, and sync flows when Supabase is configured by the app operator.
  • To improve reliability through local logging of recoverable app errors and optional remote crash telemetry when a production diagnostics service is configured by the app operator.

📍 Location Usage

  • Foreground location is used only during active trip tracking.
  • Background location permission may be requested only after the user starts the GPS tracking flow and chooses to keep trip tracking reliable while the app is not visible.
  • Background location is not used for ads, analytics, or passive monitoring.
  • Trip tracking can be stopped by the user at any time.

📷 Camera Usage

  • Camera permission is used only for odometer scanning and optional vehicle image capture.

🔔 Notifications

  • Notifications are used for maintenance reminders, system updates, and active trip tracking.
  • Weekly Reports are optional and can also remain available only inside the app without email delivery.

🗃 Data Storage

  • Core app data is stored locally on the device using Room and DataStore.
  • Backup and restore are user-initiated and stored in files chosen by the user.
  • If cloud backup is enabled, account identifiers and garage backup payloads are stored through Supabase.
  • If Weekly Reports are enabled, report preferences and in-app report history may also be stored locally and may sync to configured backend infrastructure for optional delivery features.

🔗 Data Sharing

  • SteerIn does not intentionally sell personal data.
  • Data is only sent to configured third-party services required for app functionality, such as Supabase for optional cloud auth/sync, Google location/maps services for trip tracking and workshop maps, Logo.dev for optional vehicle brand imagery, and an optional crash diagnostics provider if enabled by the app operator.
  • Optional weekly report emails may use a transactional email provider configured by the app operator. These emails contain summary data only and do not include raw GPS route coordinates.

🔒 User Control

  • Users can edit or delete vehicles and maintenance data.
  • Users can clear all local data from the app.
  • Users can export and restore local backup files.
  • Users can sign out of cloud sync at any time.
  • Users can delete the current cloud backup from Settings without deleting local device data.
  • Users can disable Weekly Reports at any time from Settings.
  • Cloud account deletion support should still be requested through the app operator until self-service account deletion is shipped.
